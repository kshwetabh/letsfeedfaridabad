## 1. What is Let's Feed Faridabad?

Let's Feed Faridabad (LFF) is a group of volunteers from different backgrounds ranging from students to professionals, doctors to businessman and elderly people. With the firm belief that a healthy country starts from a healthy society, we strive to help the poor, needy people, educate the underprivileged kids and support the elderly staying at old age homes.
